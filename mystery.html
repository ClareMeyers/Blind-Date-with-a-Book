Mystery

Mystery Book 1

About the book

A high school student sets out to prove a closed murder case is, in fact, not closed, for her capstone project. Yet, as she delves into the intricacies of the case, she begins to unearth a complicated web of deception, danger, and illegal activity that threatens to shatter the town's idyllic exterior and makes her the next target.

Mystery Book 2

About the book

A middle-aged woman's life takes an unexpected turn when her husband suddenly disappears, leaving behind a cryptic note and a teenage daughter. As the woman tries to make sense of his disappearance, she and her stepdaughter find themselves smack in the middle of a secret and dangerous past.

Mystery Book 3

About the book

A struggling author is offered the opportunity to ghostwrite a successful book series after the original author was injured in an accident. However, as she delves into the previous author's notes, she discovers a hidden autobiography revealing dark and disturbing secrets.

Mystery Book 4

About the book

A man is released from prison after serving a ten-year sentence for an unknown crime that occurred when he was a student at an elite arts conservatory. On the day he's released, he's greeted by the retired detective who imprisoned him, and after some convincing, he finally agrees to tell the detective the full story of what really happened ten years ago.

Mystery Book 5

About the book

A woman who grew up poor in the marshes of North Carolina becomes the primary suspect in the murder of a local man. Hated and misunderstood by the town, the woman fights for her innocence in court, but nothing is quite what it seems.
