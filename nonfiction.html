Mystery Book 1

About the book

In this autobiographical memoir, a neurosurgeon diagnosed with terminal lung cancer reflects on the meaning of life, death, and the search for meaning.

This book is When Breath Becomes Air by Paul Kalanithi!

Mystery Book 2

About the book

After a series of tragedies and poor life choices, one woman decides to drop everything and hike over 1,000 miles along the scenic West Coast. Confronting the physical challenges of the trail becomes a means to grapple with her inner demons, achieve forgiveness for her past, and find the strength to change her life around.

This book is Wild by Cheryl Strayed!

Mystery Book 3

About the book

A former child actor opens up about her life growing up in the spotlight, including mental health struggles, addiction, and a complex relationship with her emotionally abusive mother. It's only after her mother dies of cancer that she gets a taste of freedom, initiating a journey of recovery to seize control of her life for the first time.

This book is I'm Glad my Mom Died by Jennette McCurdy

Mystery Book 5

About the book

In the 1950s, a Black woman's cancer cells became the foundation for numerous medical breakthroughs after being illegally taken from her without her consent. This memoir tells her story and follows the author's journey as she investigates the woman's life and legacy.

This book is The Immortal Life of Henrietta Lacks by Rebecca Skloot
