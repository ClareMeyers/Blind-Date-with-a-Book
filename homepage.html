Welcome to "Blind Date with a Book" — where we take the phrase “don’t judge a book by its cover” literally.

About Us

Bookstores, libraries, and publishers have been using the "blind date with a book" concept to encourage people to try out new books for years. Traditionally, the books are wrapped in plain paper that hides the book's cover, and come with a brief description and some other clues about the book's contents.

By removing the assumptions people make based on a book's cover image, title, and author, "blind date with a book" can lead to participants falling in love with books they would never have picked up otherwise. Plus, it's fun!

Unfortunately, not every bookstore offers a "blind date with a book" experience, and readers may occasionally find themselves unintentionally repurchasing a book they've already read.

So, to remedy these issues, we created Blind Date with a Book (the online version), which allows readers everywhere to participate in the activity from the comfort of their homes.

We've divided all our mystery book candidates into one of the following four genres: romance, mystery, fantasy & science fiction, and nonfiction. So, whether you're a romantic at heart, a mystery aficionado, a dreamer of fantastical worlds, or a seeker of real-life stories, we have a roster of candidates perfect for whatever you're searching for!

Here's how it works:

1. Click on one of the images below, and you'll be taken to a new page with books in that genre.

2. Once you're there, you'll see a brief synopsis of each book, and you will be able to learn even more about it by clicking a series of buttons that reveal key tropes, quotes, and information about the author.

3. Once you've clicked all the buttons, the blank cover hiding the book will disappear, and your perfect match will be revealed!

4. Purchase the book online or in person, and enjoy your date!
