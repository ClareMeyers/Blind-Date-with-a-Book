Fantasy & Sci-Fi

Mystery Book 1

About the book

A young disabled woman is forced to enroll in a war college that trains dragon riders, but she must keep her disability a secret or risk getting killed for being a liability. Yet with every day that passes, she struggles to stay in one piece as she faces deadly trials, uncovers deadly secrets, and makes a powerful enemy out of a —conveniently handsome —dragon rider.

This book is Fourth Wing by Rebecca Yarros!

Mystery Book 2

About the book

An adolescent male heir to an intergalactic noble family travels to a desert planet to oversee the harvesting of a valuable spice. However, things quickly go awry after his arrival, and he suddenly finds himself struggling to survive as he is stranded on the foreign planet.

This book is Dune by Frank Herbert!

Mystery Book 3

About the book

A teenage prisoner and former assassin is given a chance at freedom when the Crown Prince offers her a deal: compete in a deadly tournament to earn her freedom. Taken to the royal court and forced to play pretend while competing, she becomes entangled in political intrigue, dark forces, and a destiny that transcends her life as an assassin.

This book is Throne of Glass by Srah J Maas!

Mystery Book 4

About the book

In this loose reimagining of "Romeo and Juliet" set in 1920s Shanghai, a teenage girl is the heir to the city's top gang. However, when a string of mysterious monster attacks start threatening the city, she is forced to team up with the heir to a rival gang to solve the mystery and protect their people. Mayhem, mystery, magic, and romance ensue.

This book is These Violent Delights by Chloe Gong!

Mystery Book 5

About the book

A continent is threatened by the reawakening of an ancient evil dragon, but the East and West provinces would rather fight each other than unite against the evil to come. Three women must ultimately work together to ally the provinces, stop the reawakening, and save their world. Lots of magic and political intrigue ensues.

This book is The Priory of the Orange Tree by Samantha Shannon!
